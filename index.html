Bible Map

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2026

Bible Map does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal information. There are no accounts, no servers, and no analytics. Everything you track stays on your device unless you choose to export it. That's the whole product philosophy, not just a policy.

What information the app stores

The only information Bible Map keeps is the reading progress you create by tapping chapters — which chapters you've marked as read and the date and time you marked them. This is used solely to draw your heatmap, streaks, and stats.

This information is stored locally on your device. It is never sent to us or to any third party, because there is no "us" server for it to be sent to — Bible Map has no backend.

What we do not collect

We want to be specific, because "we value your privacy" statements usually aren't. Bible Map does not:

  • Require or offer an account, login, email, or password.
  • Collect your name, email address, location, contacts, photos, or device identifiers.
  • Include any analytics, crash-reporting, advertising, or tracking software (there are no third-party tracking SDKs in the app).
  • Make any network requests to transmit your reading data. The app functions fully offline.

Backups you create

Bible Map lets you export a backup of your reading progress as a file. When you do this:

  • The backup is a plain file that you control. You choose where it goes using your device's standard share sheet — for example saving it to Files, iCloud Drive, or sending it to yourself.
  • We never see this file. It is handed directly to the destination you pick.
  • If you later import a backup, the app reads the file you select and merges it into your local progress. That file is processed entirely on your device.

Once a backup leaves the app, it is subject to the privacy terms of wherever you chose to store it (for example, Apple's iCloud terms). We have no visibility into or control over that.

Purchases

Bible Map is a one-time paid app. Payment is processed entirely by the Apple App Store (or the Google Play Store). We never receive your payment details, credit card number, or billing address. Any information involved in your purchase is handled by Apple or Google under their own privacy policies: Apple · Google.

Children's privacy

Bible Map does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). Because no data is collected or transmitted, the app poses no data-collection risk to children.

Your control over your data

Because your data lives only on your device, you are always in full control:

  • Export it at any time from the app.
  • Delete it by clearing your progress in the app or by uninstalling Bible Map, which removes all locally stored reading data from your device.

We cannot delete your data for you, because we never have it.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above and post the revised policy at the same URL. Because the app collects no data, we do not expect material changes; any update would simply add clarity.
